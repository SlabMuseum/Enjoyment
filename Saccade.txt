1. Low Saccade Rate (~0 - 0.5 saccades/sec) → High Engagement
Indicates longer fixations and sustained attention on an object.
Suggests deep visual processing—the participant is absorbed in the artwork.
Common in aesthetic appreciation and concentration on details.
2. Moderate Saccade Rate (~0.5 - 1.5 saccades/sec) → Neutral Engagement
A balance between exploration and focus.
The participant is looking around but still processing details.
Could indicate moderate interest or searching for meaning.
3. High Saccade Rate (>1.5 saccades/sec) → Low Engagement or Uncertainty
Indicates frequent gaze shifts, suggesting disinterest, distraction, or uncertainty.
May imply the participant did not find the object engaging enough to fixate on it.
Could also indicate confusion or difficulty in interpretation.
